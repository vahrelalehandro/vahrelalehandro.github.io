Fried Egg

Ingredients :

  • egg
  • salt and pepper
  • oil

Steps :

  1. prepare pan , heat it and pour some oil
  2. break the egg and pour into pan
  3. wait until 2 minute and then spread some salt and pepper
  4. turn side of egg and wait for several second
  5. after finished, put the egg to plate
  6. it is ready to serve
